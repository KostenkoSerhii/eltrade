What is e-currency?

E-currencies - Electronic Currency refers to money or scrip which is only exchanged electronically. Usually it means the use of computer networks, the Internet and digital stored value systems. Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT), direct deposit, digital gold currency and virtual currency are all examples of electronic money. Also, it is a collective term for financial cryptography and technologies enabling it
